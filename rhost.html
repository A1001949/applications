Restaurant Host

A team management and scheduling tool that makes coordinating meetings for school projects instantaneous.

Problem Statement

Coordinating face-to-face meetings based on everyone's weekly schedules is difficult and time-consuming for undergraduate students.

By connecting with existing university infrastructures, Avail effectively provides optimal time-slot suggestions for productive meetings.
