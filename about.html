My name is
Adrian Wong

I am a Master's candidate at the University of Toronto, studying User Experience and Human-Centered Data Science. With my multidisciplinary background in Psychology, Digital Media, and Computer Science, I've developed a genuine passion for designing delightful user experiences.

Currently, I work as a Research Assistant at KMDI-Semaphore Labs, located within the University of Toronto. In particular, we are examining the integration of AI and reality-enhancing technologies (including VR, AR, MR, and XR) in the lives of youth.

In my free time, I enjoy trying new foods, hiking, watching movies, and playing video games with my friends.
