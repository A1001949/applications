Financial Disclosure

A platform that simplifies the financial disclosure process for clients while promoting productive collaboration with other professionals.

Project Brief

Financial disclosure is typically the most time consuming, expensive, and unpleasant process in dispute resolution. Simplifying this processes on a professional platform helps clients reduce complications, administrative headaches, and liability risks, all while increasing productivity.

Project Duration

14 weeks
(May - Aug 2019)

Team Size

Design team of 2
(including me)

My Responsibilities

User Research, Wireframes, User Flows, Prototypes, Analysis
