Message Centre

Empowering customers to stay informed about their account, wherever and whenever.

Case Overview

Following our mobile-first strategy, we discovered that there was an opportunity to keep customers informed on the mobile app.

After a collaborative design sprint, we conducted usability tests to determine which of two designs users found to be more intuitive.
