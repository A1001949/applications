Message Centre

Empowering customers to stay informed about their account, wherever and whenever.

Case Overview

Throughout the 2020 pandemic, customers called Capital One CSRs at a time when non-digital channels were overloaded and costly.

By applying an empowerment model, we can help customers self-serve card management and security features via digital engagement.
